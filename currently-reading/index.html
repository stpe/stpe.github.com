Currently Reading

Install Chrome Extension from Chrome Web Store

Stay motivated and on track with your reading - see what you are reading, and what inspiring books you have lined up everytime you open a new window in your browser.

This extension retrieves your reading status from your Literal profile. If you don't have an account, sign up - it is free.
